Climate Change in the Contiguous
United States (1895–2019)

CISC 375 Web Development
Group: Breanna, Keng, Kayla

This project explores how temperatures across the contiguous United States have changed from 1895–2019, using data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration (NOAA). The dataset was featured in The Washington Post’s “2°C: Beyond the Limit” series.

You can explore:

  • State-level temperature trends
  • Top warming counties
  • Seasonal temperature changes
